About Us

Diet Recommendation

This project analyzes individual health data, including dietary habits, physical activity, and medical history, to develop a personalized nutritional diet profile.

It uses an algorithm to generate tailored diet plans, recommending specific macro elements needed for body and portion sizes that align with the user's health goals

The main goal is to promote sustainable healthy eating by offering actionable, easy-to-follow, and evidence-based dietary recommendations.
